What is the FNFTA?

The First Nations Financial Transparency Act makes audited financial statements and remuneration schedules publicly available.

What does OpenBand show?

Chief and Council remuneration, travel, expenses, credit card amounts, other payments, and totals when the PDF parser can read them.

Why pending?
