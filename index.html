What is the FNFTA?

The First Nations Financial Transparency Act requires First Nations to publish audited financial statements and Chief & Council remuneration schedules on ISC's website.

⌕

What does OpenBand show?

OpenBand displays parsed remuneration rows when available and links to the original ISC PDF for verification.

Data availability

Some PDFs are posted but not parsed yet. When a newer year is empty, OpenBand now shows the newest parsed year by default.
